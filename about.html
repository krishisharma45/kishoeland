kishoeland: the story

I started kishoeland.com as a way to host the story I have been writing. It evaluates the effect of Artificial Intelligence and the challenges that our society faces as a result of automation through the lens of a children's story.


This story is meant to educate, entertain, and inspire us to grow young, like Luv and Kishoe. More than ever, we need to be brave, not afraid; encouraged, not shamed.


Follow Luv + Kishoe as they explore Kishoeland. If you have any thoughts or comments, message me on Instagram, @kishoeland
