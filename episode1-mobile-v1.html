Luv At First Sight

Episode 1


Kishoe was a young artist who lived in Kishoeland. She was an optimist with big ideas.


One day, Kishoe went out for a walk. She was lost in her imagination, when suddenly, she bumped into someone.


“Hi, I’m Luv, like the emotion,” he responded. He told her he was an engineer. He was a cynic with big dreams.


They walked together and quickly became friends.
