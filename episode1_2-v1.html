Luv and Kishoe talked all the time. They held their most important discussions at their favorite secret spot, Twilight Twin Hills.


"Adult life is boring. They work all the time. I want to be a kid forever," said Kishoe.


Luv agreed. "Yeah, adults look tired and stressed. I wish we didn't have to grow up. I want to do cool things with my life, not work at a boring bank or company," said Luv.


That was when Kishoe had an idea - an awesome idea - that would save them from the woes of adulthood.
