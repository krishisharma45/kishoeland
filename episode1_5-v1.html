Luv called Kishoe later that night. He wanted to make sure his friend was okay.


"Kishoe, I'm sorry if I hurt you. Your idea is important. I just wanted to help you make a good plan. I really want your idea to succeed," Luv told her.


"Thanks Luv," Kishoe said. She smiled - she had another idea! She knew what she really needed to bring her idea to life - or rather, who.


"I need Luv," Kishoe thought. Because with Luv, you can do anything (or so they say). Kishoe asked Luv to join her, and he eagerly agreed.


And so, from that day on, Kishoe and Luv teamed up to bring an end to adulthood.
