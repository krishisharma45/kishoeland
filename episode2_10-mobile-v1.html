Kishoe explained the benefits of automation to the audience. This time, she had their full attention. She gave the crowd her pitch.


“… and yes, change is scary,” Kishoe wrapped up, “but these robots can help us focus on more important things. For every one job that this robot takes away, it creates three new, more exciting jobs. We want to start the discussion now, and need your help. Thank you for your time!”


The crowd clapped. Luv and Kishoe smiled. Everything felt right. Nothing could hurt their idea now.


In the back of the crowd, Boz stood, glaring at, what he thought, was just child’s play. He pulled out his phone and dialed.


“President, we’ve got a problem,” he said in a low voice.
