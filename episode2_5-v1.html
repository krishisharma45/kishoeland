The crowd’s laughter grew louder and louder. Kishoe felt her confidence plummet. She couldn’t understand what was so funny.


“Robots can’t replace us!” said a wo-blob in the crowd.


“This is silly. Come back with a real idea,” shouted a ma-blob.


Kishoe tried continuing her pitch, but the crowd was out of her control. She saw blobs leaving.


Kishoe searched for love - sorry, Luv. The crowd was heartless.


And Luv-less. He was nowhere to be seen.
